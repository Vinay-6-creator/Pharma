Why Internships Matter

Internships are crucial for pharmacy students as they provide:

  • Practical experience in real pharmaceutical environments
  • Networking opportunities with industry professionals
  • Enhanced resume for job applications
  • Potential job offers from internship hosts
  • Understanding of career preferences and specializations
  • Development of professional skills and confidence

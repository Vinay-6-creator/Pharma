Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 11, 2026

At PharmaX, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number)
  • Educational background and academic records
  • Profile information and preferences
  • Messages and communications you send us
  • Payment information (if applicable)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you access our platform, we automatically collect:

  • Device information (browser type, IP address, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, links clicked)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Location information (if permitted)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Send educational updates and notifications
  • Process applications for internships and opportunities
  • Communicate with you about your account

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Partner organizations for internship opportunities (with your consent)
  • Service providers who assist us in operating our platform
  • Law enforcement when required by law
  • Other users when you choose to share information publicly

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to access, update, or correct your personal information at any time through your account settings.

5.2 Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at privacy@pharmk.in

5.3 Opt-Out

You can opt-out of receiving marketing communications by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails.

5.4 Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

PharmaX is designed for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete this information promptly.

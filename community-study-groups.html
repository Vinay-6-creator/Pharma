✨ Benefits of Study Groups

🤝

Peer Learning

Learn from your peers' perspectives and explanations. Teaching others reinforces your own understanding.

📚

Shared Resources

Access notes, practice papers, and study materials compiled and shared by the group members.

💡

Problem Solving

Tackle difficult problems together and explore multiple solution approaches from different perspectives.

🎯

Motivation & Accountability

Stay motivated with regular study schedules and be accountable to your study group members.

🏆

Better Grades

Studies show students in study groups score 15-20% higher than those studying alone.

🌐

Networking

Build lasting connections with fellow pharmacy students and potentially future colleagues.

🚀 How Study Groups Work

1. Choose Your Group

Select a study group based on your subject interests and learning goals. You can join multiple groups!

2. Attend Meetings

Join weekly video meetings with your group. Discussions are scheduled at convenient times for all members.

3. Share & Learn

Present topics, share notes, ask questions, and help others. Collaboration is key to success.

4. Track Progress

Monitor your progress and achievements in each study group with badges and certificates.

D Pharm - Year 2

Community Pharmacy & Professional Practice

Pharmacy operations, patient counseling, and professional responsibility in community settings.

I

Community Pharmacy Operations

•
Pharmacy Layout: Organization, storage areas, dispensary setup
•
Inventory Management: Stock control, expiry date tracking, ordering systems
•
Accounting & Finance: Pricing, billing, profit margins, financial records

❓ Important Previous Year Questions

Q1. Explain inventory management in community pharmacy
Answer: Stock maintenance to meet demand, expiry tracking, FIFO method (First In First Out), regular audits, preventing wastage, maintaining optimal stock levels for fast and slow-moving items.
II

Patient Counseling & Communication

•
Counseling Principles: Empathy, listening, patient-centered approach
•
Drug Information Provision: Indications, dosage, side effects, interactions
•
Medication Adherence: Improving compliance, reminder systems

❓ Important Previous Year Questions

Q1. How should a pharmacist counsel a patient on antibiotic use?
Answer: Complete the full course even if better, take with/without food as specified, report allergies/side effects, avoid self-medication, proper storage instructions, finish all tablets.
III

Regulatory & Legal Compliance

•
Pharmacy Act & Rules: Licensing, premises requirements, conduct standards
•
Drug Regulations: Scheduled drugs, prescription requirements, record-keeping
•
Professional Ethics: Confidentiality, responsible practice, patient rights

❓ Important Previous Year Questions

Q1. What are Schedule H drugs and how should they be handled?
Answer: Habit-forming drugs (antibiotics, antihistamines). Requirements: Valid prescription, record-keeping, proper storage, limited quantity sale, patient counseling on potential dependence.
IV

Health Promotion & Disease Prevention

•
Public Health Initiatives: Immunization, health camps organization
•
Disease Management Programs: Diabetes, hypertension screening
•
Lifestyle Counseling: Diet, exercise, stress management education

❓ Important Previous Year Questions

Q1. Discuss the role of pharmacist in promoting public health
Answer: Disease screening, health awareness campaigns, vaccination support, lifestyle modifications counseling, medication therapy management, chronic disease counseling programs.

📚 B.PHARM SYLLABUS - SEMESTER WISE IMPORTANT NOTES
===================================================================
Pharmacy Council of India (Regulations 2014, CBCS)
===================================================================

🎯 PROGRAM STRUCTURE:
- Duration: 8 Semesters (4 Academic Years)
- Total Credits: 208
- Minimum Attendance: 80% in each course
- Medium of Instruction: English

===================================================================
SEMESTER I (Odd Semester - June/July to November/December)
===================================================================

COURSES:

1. BP101T - Human Anatomy and Physiology I – Theory (3L+1T, 4 Credits)
   KEY TOPICS:
   • Introduction to human body and homeostasis
   • Cell biology and tissues
   • Cardiovascular system
   • Respiratory system
   • Nervous system basics

2. BP102T - Pharmaceutical Analysis I – Theory (3L+1T, 4 Credits)
   KEY TOPICS:
   • Analytical chemistry fundamentals
   • Gravimetric analysis
   • Volumetric analysis
   • Titrimetry principles
   • Acid-base titrations

3. BP103T - Pharmaceutics I – Theory (3L+1T, 4 Credits)
   KEY TOPICS:
   • Introduction to pharmaceutics
   • Pharmaceutical processes
   • Particle size and distribution
   • Powders
   • Granules and granulation

4. BP104T - Pharmaceutical Inorganic Chemistry – Theory (3L+1T, 4 Credits)
   KEY TOPICS:
   • Atomic structure and chemical bonding
   • Periodic table
   • Inorganic pharmaceuticals
   • Electrolytes and non-electrolytes
   • Dosage forms of inorganic drugs

5. BP105T - Communication Skills – Theory (2L, 2 Credits)*
   KEY TOPICS:
   • Professional communication
   • Written communication
   • Presentation skills
   • Interpersonal communication

6. BP106RBT/RMT - Remedial Biology/Mathematics – Theory (2L, 2 Credits)*
   • For students requiring remedial support

PRACTICAL COURSES:
- BP107P: Human Anatomy and Physiology Practical (4P, 2 Credits)
- BP108P: Pharmaceutical Analysis I Practical (4P, 2 Credits)
- BP109P: Pharmaceutics I Practical (4P, 2 Credits)
- BP110P: Pharmaceutical Inorganic Chemistry Practical (4P, 2 Credits)
- BP111P: Communication Skills Practical (2P, 1 Credit)*

TOTAL CREDITS: 27-30

===================================================================
✅ ASSESSMENT SCHEME (Per Semester)
===================================================================

Internal Assessment:
- Class Tests/Quizzes: 5 marks
- Assignments: 5 marks
- Practical Evaluation: 10 marks
Total Internal: 20 marks (Theory), 10 marks (Practical)

End Semester Examinations:
- Theory Exam: 80 marks (3 hours)
- Practical Exam: 40 marks (4 hours)
Total External: 120 marks

TOTAL PER COURSE: 150 marks

📊 GRADING SYSTEM
===================================================================

Grade | Grade Points | Percentage
------|--------------|------------
  A+  |     10       |  90-100%
  A   |     9        |  80-89%
  B+  |     8        |  70-79%
  B   |     7        |  60-69%
  C   |     6        |  50-59%
  P   |     5        |  40-49%
  F   |     0        |  <40%
  AB  |     0        |  Absent

SGPA = Σ(Credit × Grade Point) / Σ Credits
CGPA = (Σ SGPA of all 8 semesters × corresponding credits) / Total Credits

===================================================================
📋 IMPORTANT REGULATIONS
===================================================================

1. ATTENDANCE: Minimum 80% required in each course
2. ELIGIBILITY: 
   - I-II sem courses must be completed before attending III sem
   - III-IV sem courses must be completed before attending V sem
   - V-VI sem courses must be completed before attending VII sem
3. PASS CRITERIA: Minimum 50% in each course (40% with internal)
4. PRACTICE SCHOOL: 150 hours (Semester VII)
5. PROJECT: 8 Credits (Semester VIII)

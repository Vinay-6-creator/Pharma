💡 How to Ask Great Questions

1

Be Specific

Clearly describe what you don't understand. Include the exact topic, context, and what you've tried so far.

2

Provide Context

Share relevant information like the chapter number, textbook reference, or exam type to help mentors guide you better.

3

Use Tags

Tag your question with relevant subjects like Pharmacology, Chemistry, Pathology to reach the right experts.

4

Show Your Work

If applicable, show what steps you've already taken. This helps mentors understand where you're stuck exactly.

5

Follow Up

Thank helpful answerers and provide feedback. This creates a positive community culture for everyone.

6

Mark Best Answer

Once you understand, mark the most helpful answer. This guides others and rewards the answerer with badges.
